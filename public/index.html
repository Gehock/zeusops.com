Zeus Operations

Zeus Operations is an Arma focused gaming community. We have a semi-realistic playstyle and play Arma first-person only. Our aim is fun and immersive combined arms gameplay with mods like RHS, CUP, ACE and TFAR.

When do we play?

We hold events every FRIDAY, SATURDAY and SUNDAY, at 18:30 CEST for up to 2 hours with 20-40 players. Everyone has the ability to play a different role every event: tanks, helicopters, jets, infantry, or leading roles.

Interested?

Contact a staff member on our TeamSpeak or Discord. If you meet the requirements you will be given an introduction to the community and you will get set up with the mods, TeamSpeak, Discord, etc. No training or application required.

Jointops?

If your group is interested in doing PvE jointops, contact a staff member on our TeamSpeak or Discord.
